What is Uncode and what is included with my purchase?

Uncode is a famous WordPress website builder that empowers you to successfully build a professional website without writing a single line of code using its drag-and-drop interface, dozens of modules, and import pre-build carefully crafted designs. With your purchase of Uncode WordPress Theme, you will receive:

  • A copy of the latest version of Uncode
  • A lifetime Envato License to activate the product.
  • A powerful Page Builder with Backend and Frontend editors.
  • 100+ Page Builder modules and design elements.
  • 90+ demo websites that can be imported and modified.
  • 750+ Wireframe layouts that can be combined to create a web page.
  • Bundled Premium plugins (including Slider Revolution and LayerSlider).
  • 6 months of free professional support that can be extended if needed.
  • Detailed documentation with 50+ narrated video tutorials.
  • Access to all future updates, including all new features, templates, and fixes.

Are there any recurring fees?

No, absolutely not! There are no recurring fees since Uncode is based on a one-time purchase model with lifetime updates and 6 months of support at no extra costs. In fact, when you invest in Uncode, you acquire the theme and secure lifetime updates without any additional charges, ensuring that your website stays up-to-date with the latest features and improvements over the years. To further enhance your experience, we offer an initial 6 months of support as part of your purchase. After this period, should you desire continued support, you have the option to extend it. However, it’s important to note that this extension is not mandatory.

Are all the prebuilt websites included and customizable?

Yes! With a single purchase of Uncode, you gain access to our entire collection of current and future prebuilt website templates! You can import any of them at any time, eliminating the need to make an immediate decision on which one to choose. In a nutshell, you have at your disposal all the demos shown above. Our prebuilt websites are meticulously crafted by our design team, expertly showcasing Uncode’s capabilities while saving you time. These designs are fully customizable and can be mixed and matched with each other seamlessly to suit your preferences.

Do I need to know coding to build my website?

No coding skills are required to build your new website using Uncode! With our intuitive drag-and-drop Page Builder, you can craft and personalize your website precisely to your liking. Additionally, you can choose between the lightning-fast Backend Editor or the amazing Frontend Builder that allows you to edit your pages on the frontend of your website. For those with advanced expertise, further customization is possible through CSS overrides and custom coding, providing the flexibility to fine-tune your website to your specific needs. To see examples of high-quality real websites created by our customers with Uncode, and get inspiration for what you could create, please visit the Showcase.

Do you offer and how can I get support?

Of course! We offer support for all users for 6 months from the purchase date; the support period can be extended to 12 months, and, if it is necessary, it is also renewable over time. If you need private support, we have a dedicated Help Center. Here, our support experts are ready and glad to assist you if you have any doubts or require our assistance. To request support, you just need to create a ticket using the ‘Submit a Ticket’ button. The Help Center also includes detailed and extended documentation, frequently asked questions and narrated video tutorials. If your support period has expired, you can use the public Uncode Comments section on the Envato Market website. Furthermore, if you want to discuss about your project with other users, on our dedicated Facebook Group you can find a great community of Uncode users!
